ojira Settings

You can generate a PAT from your Jira profile under Personal Access Tokens. This token will be stored locally and synced across your browsers where you are logged in with the same Google account.

Enter the base URL of your Jira instance (e.g., https://your-company.atlassian.net). Do not include trailing slashes.

Select the Ollama model you want to use for generating descriptions. Ensure the model is available in your local Ollama instance. You can refresh the list if you have recently added new models to Ollama.

Security Note: Keep your PAT secure. While this extension stores it in your browser\"s synchronized storage, ensure your browser and account are secure.
